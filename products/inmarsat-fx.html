Inmarsat Fleet Xpress

Global High-Speed Broadband for Maritime Operations

Overview

Inmarsat Fleet Xpress (FX) is a high-speed maritime broadband service combining Ka-band and L-band connectivity for reliable global coverage. It provides seamless connectivity for commercial shipping, offshore operations, and maritime enterprises requiring consistent high-bandwidth communications.

Features & Benefits

  • Dual-band Technology: Combines Ka-band high-speed with L-band backup for uninterrupted service
  • Global Coverage: Seamless connectivity across all major shipping routes and remote areas
  • Flexible Data Plans: Scalable bandwidth options to match operational requirements
  • SOLAS Compliant: Meets international maritime safety requirements
  • Fleet Management: Enables real-time vessel tracking, monitoring, and operational efficiency
  • Crew Welfare: Supports crew communications, entertainment, and connectivity

Ideal For:

  • Offshore oil & gas operations

  • Government and defense maritime operations
