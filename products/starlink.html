Starlink Maritime

High-Speed Low Earth Orbit (LEO) Satellite Internet for Maritime Vessels

Overview

Starlink Maritime provides high-speed, low-latency satellite internet connectivity specifically designed for maritime use, catering to various vessels such as yachts, commercial ships, oil rigs, and ferries. Operating through Starlink's extensive constellation of Low Earth Orbit (LEO) satellites, it offers global coverage across most maritime regions.

Features & Benefits

  • Reliable In-Motion Connectivity: Designed to provide stable internet even in high-vibration environments and while vessels are moving
  • Real-Time Navigation & Operations: Facilitates real-time weather updates and monitoring, enhancing safety and efficiency
  • Crew Welfare: Enables crew members to stay connected with family, stream entertainment, and engage in online gaming
  • Fleet Management: Supports remote teams, data transfer, real-time analytics, and secure fleet management
  • End-to-End Encryption: Starlink employs encryption to safeguard user data and ensure confidentiality
  • Flexible Plans: Month-to-month plans with the ability to pause service as needed

Limitations & Considerations

  • Consistent connectivity relies on clear sky visibility
  • Signal disruptions may occur during severe weather or significant vessel movement
  • Usage in territorial waters may depend on government approvals
