Inmarsat FleetBroadband

Reliable Maritime Broadband & Voice Communications

Overview

Inmarsat FleetBroadband (FBB) is a proven maritime communications service providing simultaneous voice and broadband data services globally. It offers reliable connectivity for vessels of all sizes, from small fishing boats to large commercial ships.

Key Specifications

Data Speed

Up to 432 kbps
(Depending on terminal)

Voice

3 Voice Lines
(Simultaneous)

Coverage

Global
(Except Poles)

Features & Benefits

  • Simultaneous Services: Voice, data, SMS, and fax all at the same time
  • GMDSS Compliant: Meets Global Maritime Distress and Safety System requirements
  • Flexible Terminals: FB150, FB250, and FB500 options for different vessel sizes
  • Always-On Connection: Continuous IP connectivity for operational efficiency
  • Standard IP Interface: Easy integration with existing IT systems
  • Cost-Effective: Predictable pricing with various data plan options

Terminal Options:

  • FB150: Up to 150 kbps - Ideal for small to medium vessels
  • FB250: Up to 284 kbps - Suitable for medium-sized commercial vessels
  • FB500: Up to 432 kbps - Best for larger vessels requiring higher bandwidth
