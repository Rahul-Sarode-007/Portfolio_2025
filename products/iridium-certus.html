Iridium Certus

True Global L-band Satellite Connectivity

Overview

Iridium Certus is a multi-service platform delivering truly global L-band connectivity through the Iridium satellite constellation. It provides reliable voice and data services with 100% global coverage, including polar regions, making it ideal for maritime operations in remote areas.

Features & Benefits

  • True Global Coverage: Only satellite network with 100% coverage including poles
  • Weather Resilient: L-band frequency provides reliable connectivity in all weather conditions
  • Multiple Service Classes: Certus 100, 200, 350, and 700 for different bandwidth needs
  • Safety Services: GMDSS-compliant terminals available for maritime safety
  • Compact Terminals: Small, lightweight antennas suitable for all vessel types
  • IoT & M2M: Supports Internet of Things and machine-to-machine applications

Service Classes:

  • Certus 100: Up to 88 kbps - Basic connectivity and safety services
  • Certus 200: Up to 176 kbps - Enhanced data and voice services
  • Certus 350: Up to 352 kbps - Mid-tier broadband for operational needs
  • Certus 700: Up to 704 kbps - High-speed broadband for demanding applications
