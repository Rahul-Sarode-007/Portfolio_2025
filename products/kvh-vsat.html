KVH TracPhone V-Series

High-Performance Maritime VSAT Solutions

Overview

KVH TracPhone V-Series delivers high-speed maritime VSAT connectivity through stabilized antenna systems. Designed for commercial vessels, yachts, and offshore operations, it provides reliable broadband internet and voice services with global coverage.

Features & Benefits

  • Advanced Stabilization: 3-axis gyro-stabilized antennas for reliable connectivity in rough seas
  • Compact Design: Space-efficient antennas suitable for vessels of all sizes
  • High-Speed Internet: Supports video streaming, VoIP, and data-intensive applications
  • Flexible Plans: Customizable data plans to match operational requirements
  • Easy Installation: Simplified setup and integration with existing systems
  • 24/7 Support: Global technical support and service network

TracPhone V-Series Models:

  • V3-HTS: 37cm antenna - Compact solution for smaller vessels
  • V7-HTS: 60cm antenna - Balanced performance for mid-size vessels
  • V11-HTS: 1m antenna - High-performance for larger commercial vessels
